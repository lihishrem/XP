PROJECT OVERVIEW

EXA Finance is an Algorand stationed Peer-to-Peer Multi-Asset Exchange that offers a simple and accessible way to trade Fungible and Non-Fungible Tokens.

exa.finance

The platform provides users with several features, like an inter-chain bridge that enables them to wrap and exchange assets from chains like Cardano and Solana and an easy-to-use digital wallet. One of its truly innovative features is the EXA basket, which allows users to trade up to four assets of different kinds at once - combining cryptocurrency and NFTs into one single transaction.

Isolated Islands
and the NFT
Market

The 2021 digital assets and NFT boom brought awareness to a nutritious obstacle in the blockchain world, also known as "The isolated island problem" - where a specific blockchain community can only perform transactions within itself but without access beyond its boundaries.
As digital assets trade became an integral part of the blockchain world, the demand for NFT oriented tools that bypass this problem became prominent. EXA Finance is building a multi-asset marketplace where users can trade NFTs. Thanks to the Algorand blockchain, it's fast, secure, and sustainable. One of the final challenges standing on the way to achieving its goal is paving the trade routes that will turn isolated islands into a central marketplace for all assets from every blockchain.

"The isolated Island problem represents an area where a specific community can transact, but they do not have access to the outside world. We at EXA Finance are building a multi-asset marketplace on Algorand where users can trade NFTs.
XP.NETWORK opens the doors to a new range of users on our platform with its easy-to-integrate multichain bridge, solving the isolated Island issue."

Ted Nivan

CEO, EXA Finance

A piece that
completes the puzzle

On January 26th, EXA Finance and XP.NETWORK has mutually announced a partnership that ensured the platform's multi-chain capabilities necessary for a Multi-Asset Multi-Chain Asset Exchange. The XP.NETWORK's bridge has been part of the Algorand ecosystem since both parties announced their partnership on December 21st, 2021. By integrating the bridge into its system and combined with its several other bridging options, XP.NETWORK's bridge opens the doors to a new range of users on the EXA platform. Its easy-to-integrate multi-chain bridge solves the isolated island, attracting capital and assets from and toward over a dozen ecosystems beyond the Algorand blockchain.

"XP.NETWORK is delighted to know that other financial DeFi platforms understand the unprecedented force we discover in easy and simple inter-chain transfers. May our bridges pave the way for more and more collaborations."

Nir Blumberger

CEO & Co-Founder, XP.NETWORK
